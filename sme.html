"SME Commercial Combined Insurance"

Protecting your small or medium-sized enterprise (SME) against a range of risks is crucial for your business's success. Our SME Commercial Combined Insurance is designed to provide comprehensive coverage in a single policy, tailored to meet the unique needs of your business.

Key Features

Property Insurance : Covering your buildings, contents, and stock against risks such as fire, theft, and damage.

Liability Insurance : Protecting you against claims for injury or damage to third parties, including public and product liability.

Business Interruption : Providing financial support if your business is unable to operate due to an insured event, such as a fire or flood.

Equipment Breakdown : Covering the cost of repairing or replacing essential equipment that breaks down.

Goods in Transit : Insuring your goods while in transit, whether by road, rail, sea, or air.

Legal Expenses : Covering the cost of legal expenses incurred in defending your business against certain legal disputes.
